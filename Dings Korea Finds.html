How buying works

Just me selling a few things I brought back, super informal. No corporate checkout, no giant inventory.

Order in 4 steps

  1. DM on Instagram (or email) with what you want. Use the product name from this page or send a screenshot.
  2. Send your ZIP so I can quote item total + shipping (shipping is never in the list price).
  3. Pay with one of the SoCal-friendly options below. I hold the item once payment clears.
  4. I ship from Norwalk, usually USPS with tracking. If you’re nearby (around Norwalk or southeast LA County), message me and we can sometimes meet in public somewhere we both pick.

How people in SoCal usually pay

Most people just pay with whatever app they already have. No fancy checkout needed.

  • Venmo, super normal here, person to person, quick in SoCal.
  • Apple Cash if you both use iPhone / Wallet.
  • Cash only for in person meetups, and pick a public spot you’re ok with.

There’s no cart on this site on purpose. Stock is tiny and I check every order myself. Tip: put the handles you actually use (Venmo @, whatever) in your Instagram bio or a highlight so people stop asking every time. If DMs ever explode I can bolt on Stripe, Square, or a simple Shopify page later, same photos, less typing.

Numbers on the page are the item only. I’ll spell out your full total in the DM before you pay (shipping, anything else we need to include) so nothing’s a surprise. This isn’t automated checkout or tax software, it’s person to person.

Questions? Just DM us.

Something on this page, stuck between two tints, shipping question, whatever, just message me. I’m not a ticket system. I usually answer within a day.
